Cherry Creek Falls

Home Page

Region: Duvall
Distance: 5.5 miles
Elevation: 300 feet
Parking Pass: None

Cherry Creek Falls is a King County gem. Located in old logging forest outside of Duvall, the 25-foot, waterfall pours straight down into a lovely pool. Because it mostly follows decommissioned logging roads, the trails are fairly-smooth and the hills are quite gentle. From the trailhead, you follow a network of unsigned roads and trails.
